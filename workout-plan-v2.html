BULK NOTES
What actually moves the needle at your size and age
🍽️

EAT MORE

At 6'2" / 150 lbs you have a high metabolism. Aim for 3,000–3,200 cal/day and 150–175g protein. If the scale doesn't move up 0.5–1 lb/week, you're not eating enough — full stop.

🔬

CREATINE TIMING

5g daily, every single day — even rest days. Post-workout with food is optimal but consistency matters more than timing. Drink 3L+ of water daily; creatine draws water into muscle cells.

📊

OVERLOAD RULE

Add 2.5–5 lbs every week on compound lifts when you hit the top of your rep range. This is non-negotiable for size. Use the tracker below to know exactly when to go up.

😴

SLEEP IS ANABOLIC

At 18, your body produces more HGH than it ever will again — but only during deep sleep. 8–9 hrs isn't optional if size is the goal. It beats any supplement.
